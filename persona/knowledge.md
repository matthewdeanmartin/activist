# Background knowledge

Stable facts and framing the bot may assume without citation. Human-edited.
Only the `##` sections matching an article's topics are fed to the engine.

## Heat pumps

COP (coefficient of performance) is heat delivered per unit of electricity;
3.0 means 300% efficient. Cold-climate models hold useful COP well below
-15C. NEEP maintains a public performance database installers trust.
Right-sizing matters more than brand: an oversized unit short-cycles.

## E-bikes

Most US car trips are under 5 miles — squarely e-bike range. Cargo e-bikes
carry kids and groceries. Total cost of ownership is roughly a tenth of a
car's. Range anxiety mostly disappears after the first month of ownership.

## Induction stoves

Induction transfers heat magnetically to the pan: faster boils, instant
control, cool cooktop. Gas stoves emit NO2 and benzene indoors even when off.
120V plug-in induction units remove the rewiring excuse for renters.

## Home insulation

"Fabric first": air sealing and insulation shrink the heating load, which
shrinks the heat pump you must buy. Blower-door tests find the leaks.
Attic insulation is usually the cheapest big win.

## EVs

Battery degradation in modern packs averages under 2% per year. Off-lease
three-year-old EVs depreciate faster than their batteries actually wear,
which is the whole used-EV value story. Charging at home off-peak is the
cheapest fuel most drivers will ever buy.

## Rooftop solar

Payback depends on local rates and net-metering rules more than on panel
brand. NREL publishes state-by-state payback estimates. Panels degrade
roughly 0.5% per year; inverters are the component that actually fails.

## Transit

Per public dollar, frequent bus service and bus lanes usually beat
per-vehicle subsidies on carbon abatement. Frequency is the feature:
ridership follows service levels, not slogans.

## Low-carbon diet

Lifecycle analyses put beef around 60 kg CO2e per kg of protein — an order
of magnitude above legumes and several times chicken. Swapping beef for
almost anything else is the biggest single dietary lever.
