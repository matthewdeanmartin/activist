=== POLICY REPORT: MSTDN+ (mstdn.plus) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Usage prohibited in regions requiring age verification
- Nudity and sexually explicit content is prohibited for accounts registered on this instance
- Violent media must be marked as sensitive when posting
- No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, or casteism
- No incitement of violence or promotion of violent ideologies
- No harassment, dogpiling or doxxing of other users
- No content illegal in the United States, Germany, or Finland
- Do not share intentionally false or misleading information
- Bots must be marked as bots in their profile preferences, and automated posts should be unlisted.
- You must select the appropriate language for each post.
- Do not spam or abuse network features.
- Please read https://mpp.bus-hit.me for moderation information and best practices.
