Short description(TL;DR of the core values)
This instance is strongly focused on user privacy, we save no IP(changing your IP with a random IPv6 before it gets sent to Mastodon), are accessible via tor and yggdrasil and use no cloudflare.
We use an hilarious amount of serverpower to provide the best possible experience, please let us know of any speed shortcomings.
Free speech is a precious good, but it has its limits.
We ban bigots, racists, terfs and users that break german or international laws, objections are denied in these cases.
As of recently, we support a 5000 character limit on toots.
Core values of ieji.de
Safety
Our strongest core value is to be a safe place for everyone, if we notice any kind of harassment coming from or going towards our instances,
the offending accounts are suspended without parole.
Privacy
Our server resides near Zurich, Switzerland, known to be a privacy fortress, additionally cooperating with a provider that is also heavily focused on privacy.
Security
We use the latest security technology to prevent sensitive data from being accessed from the outside with systems like CrowdSec and implementing the latest TLS standards.
This includes not using Cloudflare at all as this would jeopardize your privacy as a huge MITM attack vector.
A sophisticated anti-ddos filter nullifies most attacks.
Daily full backups of the database and a daily incremental backup of media files are being made towards a different datacenter.
Performance
Another main goal is showcasing how fast mastodon can be by deploying it on one of the best machines money can buy in a one of a kind european datacenter.
Anonymity
The nginx webserver does not create any logs, Mastodon is being given a randomized false IPv6 each time you interact with it(we have written our own scrambling proxy for this) and we have a
tor hidden service address
. Also accessible via
Yggdrasil Network
(somehow redirects to https so it requires acceptance of our cert). Also an early supporter of WirSpeichernNicht (german for WeDontStore), a movement against the data retention law that was planned earlier in Germany, pledging not to make logs, we are also very happy that the VÜPF in Switzerland didn't happen.
Features
Whatever comes up as features at Mastodon, we implement it, we want you to have the best Mastodon experience there is, this includes:
Full Text Search via ElasticSearch, Translation Service via LibreTranslate and a relay service via AodeRelay.
Longevity
The service is online since may 8th, 2017 and is here to stay, also thanks to the continuous support from our community.
Automatic Spam/Backlink Detection
Most of our moderation effort comes from spam or backlink seo accounts, we've written a tool that based on heuristics and spam patterns either notifies the moderation team (if not 95% certain) or suspends the account, sending the reasoning with the suspension and posting it on
@bacon
. While it might be an attack surface to show the reasoning, we believe in transparency. New accounts get scanned and rescanned once for changes, and the first couple of messages get scanned for a pattern. It is all done on the same server, nohing to worry about in terms of privacy, it plain checks for patterns and common spam behaviour.
Threads.net is blocked via domain block and iptables drop as the company has a horrendous past and does not at all align with our idea of privacy and safety.
Bridgy is blocked, as we do not want to rely on such services and rather promote using compatible standards.
Bots are fine as long as they are useful
