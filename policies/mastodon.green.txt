=== POLICY REPORT: Mastodon.green (mastodon.green) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Try 30-days for free, only 1,50 € (+VAT) per month after. No algorithms or ads to waste your time. We use 20 % of membership fees to plant trees. Change your plan or cancel at any time
- Please abide by the community standards
- You can use any official EU language, Icelandic and Norwegian on this server
- No sexually explicit images or video
- No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, or casteism
- No incitement of violence or promotion of violent ideologies
- No harassment, dogpiling or doxxing of other users
- No false or misleading information
