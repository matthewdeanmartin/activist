📜 Rules:
• Use English or French to request an account
• Use English or French to report an account or a toot
• Only fill a report if one goes against the rules, it’s not really my problem if someone doesn’t agree with you
• Add comments in your reports otherwise they’ll be ignored
• If you want to report an account exterior to hostux, only do so for illegal content in France or Norway. If you don’t want to read them, mute them.
• Respect each other 🤝
• If you follow the Humans Rights (

• Don't spam ✋
• No racism, xenophobia, sexism, discrimination… ☮️
• Please don't post illegal content (or links to) in France ⛔
• For NSFW content, use the content warning or the NSFW filter, otherwise please post your toot as unlisted 🔞

• Have fun 🎉
If you're not sure about the rules whether it's because you want to sign up or post something, please contact the admin.
When you'll sign up, you'll be following a few accounts by default. The admin chose them to make your timeline more lively, but you are free to follow and unfollow anyone!
