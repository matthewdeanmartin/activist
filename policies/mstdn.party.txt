=== POLICY REPORT: Mastodon Party (mstdn.party) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Usage prohibited in regions requiring age verification
- Sexually explicit or violent content must be marked with a content warning, and media marked as sensitive.
- No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, or casteism
- No incitement of violence or promotion of violent ideologies
- No harassment, dogpiling or doxxing of other users
- Do not share information widely-known to be false and misleading
- You must specify whether your account is automated
- You must select the appropriate language for each post.
