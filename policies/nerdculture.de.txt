=== POLICY REPORT: NerdCulture (nerdculture.de) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Be friendly and polite to each other
- No hate speech, no discrimination of others, regardless of their age, gender, nationality or whatever
- No spamming of any kind.
- Don't annoy others and don't get easily annoyed by others.
- No advertisements of commercial products.
- No distribution of copyrighted material.
- Do not upload material for which you don't have the rights
- No automated posts by bots, except when you have permission to do so by the server admins. No AI (LLM) Agents (no exceptions). We want to keep the platform human, not robot. 
- Don't abuse this service or server.
- Treat other how you want to be treated by them - with respect!
- This is a service running on a private server and as a friendly user you are welcome to use this service, but in the end the server admin can cancel user accounts and will do when the user misbehaves. The admin will decide in the end. 
- Valid mail address required! You'll receive reminder mail after 6 months of inactivity to log in again. If you don't react to those mails, your account will be deleted after 7 months of inactivity. 
- Allowed languages are German and English as main languages while as the following languages are tolerated: Dutch, French, Spanish, Italian.
- This service is free of cost for you.
However offering this service does have cost implications. If you can afford some cents per week it would be great when you could contribute in sharing the server costs. Of course this voluntarily for you. 
- No free-speech extremists, no far-right extremists, no nationalists. This includes people that elect or support such political parties like AfD in Germany, Fratelli d'Italia in Italy or Rassemblement National in France, etc.. If you support these parties, this instance is not for you. 
- Alt Texts for Pictures encouraged. Please conduct descriptive texts for pictures you upload (Alt Texts). This helps visually impaired users and others enormously in their online life. 
- To prevent spam, we use an automated analysis system for new registrations.  Domains on a Allow List are approved immediately; other requests are pre-screened by a local AI assistant before a human makes a decision.
