## ABOUT / DESCRIPTION
Croeso! Tŵt is the independent, non-profit, bilingual community for Wales and the Welsh, at home and abroad. Tŵt is a part of the Social Web: an open network of independently-run social platforms. We are moderated locally, with a focus on community wellbeing, cultural preservation, and civil dialogue.

Our Core Values
Language Equality. Our app is 100% available in Cymraeg and English. If your computer or mobile device is set to Cymraeg, your Tŵt experience will be in Cymraeg.
Community Safety. Tŵt comes with effective anti-abuse tools to help you protect yourself online. Community moderators provide personal help, and we offer a clear code of conduct.
Privacy. Tŵt ensures you can follow and be followed by the people you choose. Your messages can be seen by everyone or only the people you choose. Full account deletion is always available, and you can download everything you’ve ever shared.
No data mining, no advertising. Tŵt believes the fundamental rights to expression and association are best served in a non-profit medium.
