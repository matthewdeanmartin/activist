=== POLICY REPORT: Mastodon @ SDF (mastodon.sdf.org) ===

## SERVER RULES
- This server abides by the Mastodon Covenant 
- Organizations are welcome to engage with the community in a non-commercial, non-advertising manner.
- Artists, musicians, writers and artisans are welcome to engage with the community regarding their craft.
- Do not engage in harassment, hateful content or posts of a derogatory nature.
- Do not make advertising posts for the sake of purely promoting a commercial product or service.
- Content originating from this site may not be used for machine training or other data mining activities without the individual author's consent.

## ABOUT / DESCRIPTION
https://mastodon.sdf.org
is a an open and federated instance.
It began life as a StatusNet instance in 2010 and was converted to Mastodon in May 2017.
Daily maintenance window is from 0500 - 0700 UTC.  During this time the instance
may be
unavailable.  In the event that there is extended maintenance, please see
https://mastodon.sdf.org/status.html
for details as well as history.
We host a daily VoIP chat/improv on (929)299-1269 0200 UTC
A few of our members are artists/DJs and have shows on
https://aNONradio.net
OpenMIC is open to all daily between 0300-0600 UTC
We host a weekly Amateur Radio NET Mondays at 0000 UTC echolink TECHLINK node 9229
Are you fun and creative? Join us!
