=== POLICY REPORT: mastodon.ie (mastodon.ie) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Don't Be A Ballox
- No bigotry, racism, xenophobia, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, anti-Traveller speech or any hate speech.
-  No unsolicited DMs 
- Posts with sexual imagery depicting real people must be owned by the same people or have clear consent to be used in this manner.
- We operate on a "3 strikes and you're out" basis.
