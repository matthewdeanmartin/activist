=== POLICY REPORT: Cyberplace (cyberplace.social) ===

## SERVER RULES
- No discrimination -- including racism, sexism, homophobia or transphobia.
- No incitement of violence or promotion of violent ideologies.
- No impersonation of a person or brand.  Even parodies.
- Spam. Don't join if you just want to spam links to things you represent.
- No attacking or pentesting of this instance, or any other instance.
- Each username on here should be a person, not a brand or corporation - and that person must be you, no impersonation.
- Do not join to primarily publicise or promote 'red team' tools.
- No porn or nudity.
- No posting identifiable personal information from data breaches.
- You cannot join if you are under 18 years old. 

## ABOUT / DESCRIPTION
Life from the trenches.  Cybersecurity, fandom, video games, technology, dog photos and most importantly, you.
Federated with other Fediverse servers, this server is monitored 24/7 and backed up daily.  We fully subscribe to and comply with the Mastodon Server Covenant.
The intention is to have light touch moderation and empower you to bring yourself to the table and interact.
