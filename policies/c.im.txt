=== POLICY REPORT: C.IM (c.im) ===

## SERVER RULES
- Respect the laws of the European Union, Estonia, the Netherlands, and the Isle of Man, including regulations related to hate speech, defamation, and other applicable legal standards.
- Threatening, abusive, intolerant, or illegal behavior may result in moderation actions or further consequences. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward racism, sexism, homophobia, and transphobia. All moderation decisions are final.
- The Content Warning (CW) flag must be used for NSFW content, and all NSFW media should be marked as sensitive.
- Account registrations are monitored, and accounts deemed to be spam - including those used for commercial advertising, political campaigning or propaganda, duplicate registrations, or impersonation of legal entities - may be moderated or removed.
- Harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse of other users is strictly prohibited.
- Sharing illegal or copyrighted material without proper authorization is strictly prohibited.
- Spamming, abuse, or any malicious behavior is strictly prohibited.
- Be respectful and constructive in your interactions. Personal attacks and derogatory language will not be tolerated.
- Do not share or disclose another person’s personal information without their explicit consent.
- Content that may be distressing or sensitive should include clear tags and content warnings.
- Reports of rule violations should be submitted to the moderation team for review.
- You must be at least 18 years old to use our service.

## ABOUT / DESCRIPTION
What is Mastodon?
Mastodon is a free, open-source, decentralized social network built on open web standards.
It’s part of the Fediverse — a network of interconnected servers that talk to each other using the ActivityPub protocol.
General Information
C.IM is a general-purpose Mastodon instance for English-speaking users across the globe.
It is independently operated by
Owl Owl OÜ
, a privacy-focused company based in the EU.
We fully support and comply with the
Mastodon Server Covenant
, and block certain instances to uphold its principles.
Our mission is to provide a long-term, stable, and respectful space for people to share ideas, creativity, and everyday life - with no ads, no tracking, and a strong commitment to user rights and decentralized values.
Content and Moderation Guidelines
These rules outline how we moderate content on C.IM.
They are not legal documents, but give transparency to our moderation policies. Final interpretation lies with the moderators.

🚫 Harassment and abuse
Any behavior meant to harass, stalk, dox, or degrade others — or to encourage others to do so — is prohibited. This includes:
Repeatedly contacting someone after they have asked you to stop
Publishing or sharing someone’s personal information without consent (doxxing)
Encouraging others to target someone (brigading or dogpiling)
We reserve the right to remove any account at any time if a user violates these guidelines or disrupts the community.
Media Retention
Local media
(uploaded by C.IM users) is stored indefinitely, unless the user deletes it or it is removed for violating our rules.
Remote media
(from other federated servers) is cached for up to
90 days
. It may be deleted earlier to save storage or comply with legal takedown requests.
Users may request deletion of their content or account at any time, and we will respond promptly in accordance with applicable EU data laws.
