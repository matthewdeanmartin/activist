Eligibility
You must be aged 13 years or older to register for an account. We do not require you to use any particular language whilst using the service.
Brands
The Blower is a community, not a billboard or a bullhorn. As such, we do not allow brands to register accounts. Brands are invited to join another instance or create their own – try
brands.town
. Use these guidelines for what we consider a Brand:
If you are a for-profit business, you are a brand
If you are a political party, you are a brand
If you are unsure whether you are a brand, please email
admin@theblower.au
to sort it out before registering.
Bot accounts
Bot accounts (i.e., those with automated posting) are permitted on The Blower under these additional conditions:
Post privacy must be set to "unlisted", "followers only" , or "direct"
The “This is a bot account” checkbox must be set in the profile.
The account must identify the bot owner in the bio.
Bots must not interact with (mention) other accounts unless those accounts have interacted with the bot first.
We reserve the right to refuse or revoke any user's access permissions, at any time, for any reason, except as limited by law.

Content Warning (CW) Guidelines
We encourage the use of Content Warnings (aka Content Wrappers, aka Summaries, aka Topics, aka Subject lines, hereafter CWs) on your posts. CWs are a courtesy to your readers and other members of The Blower community. CWs are not self-censorship: they do not suppress the indexing of posts, only their initial visibility.
The Blower believes that CWs are an important tool to support people's self-autonomy, but they can also be used for expression and creativity.
CWs are not expected on posts made by marginalised people about their own marginalisation, though authors may choose to do so.
Safety and Moderation
We strive to be a welcoming, and safe instance. Mastodon has safety features such as word/hashtag filtering to limit visibility to unwanted content, and we encourage the use of  block/muting other accounts rather than engaging in vitriolic arguments. Please make use of the ability to report accounts that break the rules.
Please report users who break following the code of conduct, but please do not message people on behalf of the moderation team. Instead, you can scroll on, block, mute, or report to let moderators deal with it.
We will
not act
on complaints regarding:
Criticising racist, sexist, or otherwise oppressive behaviour or assumptions.
Good faith and non-malicious conduct whose object is to ameliorate (improve) the conditions of disadvantaged individuals or groups.
Reasonable communication of boundaries, such as “leave me alone,” “go away,” or “I’m not discussing this with you”.
Swearing or profanity.
The Blower is one of many Fediverse communities, each with their own rules, moderation, and ethos. You are able to move servers in the future without losing your followers if this instance turns out to not be the best place for you.
